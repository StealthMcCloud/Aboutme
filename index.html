Who Am I?

I have many favorite video games but I would have to say any games from the RPG Genre tend to be my favorite of all of them.

I am more than happy to answer any questions that anyone may have about what I like as well. I am proud to say that the show has changed my way of thinking and that it has made me a much better person.

I also enjoy to read when I have the time. I really enjoy reading the Fallout Equestria Books as these books tend to combine two of my favorite things into one. My little Pony and Fallout. I do enjoy other books as well such as the Eragon series, Harry Potter and even the Dragon Lance books.

Finally I am very open minded. I am not one to judge anyone for what they like and I am even willing to give things a try if I haven't tried them before. It also helps to give me a better understanding of the individual and will help me to get along with them better. All in all I am a very happy open minded individual with a unique diversity of characteristics and I love to make friends.
